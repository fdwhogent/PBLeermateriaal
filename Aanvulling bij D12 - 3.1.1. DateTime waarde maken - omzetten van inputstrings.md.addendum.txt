Weg en weer van DateOnly naar en van DateTime (misschien zinvol omdat je via DateTime instanties aan TimeSpan's wil geraken):

            DateOnly someDateOnly = new DateOnly(2026, 3, 12);
            DateTime someDateTime = someDateOnly.ToDateTime(TimeOnly.MinValue);
            DateOnly otherDateOnly = DateOnly.FromDateTime(someDateTime);
            Console.WriteLine(someDateOnly);
            Console.WriteLine(someDateTime);
            Console.WriteLine(otherDateOnly);
